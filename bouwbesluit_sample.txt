Section 1. Fire Safety:
All residential buildings must have at least one smoke detector per floor. Buildings taller than 13 meters must include a secondary escape route that complies with NEN 8112 standards.

Section 2. Accessibility:
Public buildings must be accessible to wheelchair users. Entrances must be at least 90 cm wide and equipped with automatic doors.

Section 3. Energy Efficiency:
New constructions must meet BENG (Bijna Energie Neutrale Gebouwen) requirements. Solar panels must be included if roof orientation is optimal.

Section 4. Noise Insulation:
Apartments must have a minimum sound insulation value of 52 dB between units, measured in accordance with NEN 5077.
